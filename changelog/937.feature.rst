Added ``NDCube._slice_custom_state``, an overridable no-op hook called at the
end of ``NDCube.__getitem__`` with the newly sliced cube and the sanitized
slice item. Subclasses carrying extra state that tracks the data axes (e.g.
per-frame WCS objects) can override it instead of overriding ``__getitem__``
and re-deriving the normalized item themselves.
